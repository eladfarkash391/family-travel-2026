Alaska

Anchorage → Denali → Kenai Fjords → Inside Passage → Glacier Bay

Alaska in July is extraordinary — Denali visible, brown bears on salmon, humpback whales breaching, glaciers calving. North America's most dramatic wilderness, almost completely empty. Flown in via BA/AA through Seattle or Anchorage.

Key Highlights
  • Denali — North America's highest peak, 6,190m, visible 200km away
  • Brown bears fishing salmon at Brooks Falls — world-famous spectacle
  • Kenai Fjords — orca, humpbacks, sea otters from a day boat
  • Glacier Bay calving ice — thunderous, primordial
  • Inside Passage by ferry or small ship — Alaska's SE fjord system
  • Midnight sun in July — 20+ hours of daylight at Denali
